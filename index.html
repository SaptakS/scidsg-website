About Us

We are a group of educators, technologists, and artists that's worked with organizations like the World Economic Forum, EFF, The Mozilla Foundation, Freedom of the Press Foundation, Distributed Denial of Secrets, Tor Project, Riseup.net, The Calyx Institute, Signal, OONI, and more. Our products help to measure and circumvent internet censorship, give users control of their data, and provide alternatives to the software created by Big Tech. So if you're an organization or project that helps people online or off, we want to work with you.
