About Us

We are a group of educators, technologists, and artists that's worked with organizations like the World Economic Forum, EFF, The Mozilla Foundation, Freedom of the Press Foundation, Distributed Denial of Secrets, Tor Project, Riseup.net, The Calyx Institute, Signal, OONI, and more. Our products help to measure and circumvent internet censorship, give users control of their data, and provide alternatives to the software created by Big Tech. If you're an organization or project that helps people online or off, we want to work with you.

Science & Design was founded by Glenn Sorrentino. Glenn is a product designer and user researcher that's spent over a decade building products at scale for a leading Fortune 500. His work has led to setting industry standards, US patents, and invitations to collaborate with organizations and institutions like New America, Harvard, and the World Economic Forum. For nearly the same time, he provided pro-bono services to organizations like The Calyx Institute, Signal, OnionShare, EFF, and The Tor Project.
